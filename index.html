The History of Christmas

Christmas is a celebration of the birth of Jesus Christ, observed on December 25th. It is a time for family gatherings, giving, and celebrating joy across the world.

Christmas traditions have evolved over centuries. From the early Christian customs to modern-day festivities, it is celebrated in many different ways depending on culture and country.

Christmas Traditions Around the World

  • In Germany, families decorate Christmas trees and celebrate with the Advent Calendar.
  • In Mexico, the "Posadas" are reenacted, symbolizing the journey of Mary and Joseph.
  • In the United States, people decorate their homes with lights and celebrate with festive meals.
